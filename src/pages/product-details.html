About Flywheel Pro 2.0

The Flywheel Pro 2.0 represents the pinnacle of inertial training technology, designed for professional athletes and high-performance training facilities. This advanced device utilizes the principles of rotational inertia to provide variable resistance throughout the entire range of motion.

Key Benefits

  • Eccentric Overload: Targets the eccentric phase of movement for enhanced strength gains
  • Variable Resistance: Adapts to your force output for optimal training stimulus
  • Performance Tracking: Built-in sensors measure power, velocity, and force metrics
  • Versatility: Multiple attachment options for a wide range of exercises
