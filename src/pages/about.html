Our Story

Founded in 2020, TRANEX has quickly established itself as a leader in sports technology innovation. What began as a small team of engineers and athletes has grown into a global company serving professional sports teams, training facilities, and individual athletes worldwide.

Our commitment to excellence drives everything we do. From research and development to manufacturing and customer support, we strive to deliver products that exceed expectations and help athletes achieve their full potential.

Our Mission & Values

Mission

To empower athletes with innovative technology that enhances performance, prevents injuries, and optimizes training efficiency.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in sports technology, investing heavily in research and development.

Quality

We maintain the highest standards in materials, manufacturing, and testing to ensure our products deliver consistent, reliable performance.

Integrity

We operate with transparency and honesty in all our business practices, building trust with our customers, partners, and employees.
